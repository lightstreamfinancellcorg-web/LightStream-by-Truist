About LightStream by Truist

Who We Are

LightStream by Truist is a leading online lending platform dedicated to providing fast, affordable, and transparent loan solutions to individuals and businesses across the United States. With over 15 years of experience in the financial services industry, we have helped thousands of customers achieve their financial goals.

We believe in making lending simple, transparent, and accessible to everyone. Our streamlined online application process, competitive interest rates, and exceptional customer service set us apart from traditional lenders.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and businesses with accessible, affordable financial solutions that help them achieve their dreams and build a brighter future.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and customer-centric online lending platform in America, known for our innovation, integrity, and commitment to financial inclusion.
